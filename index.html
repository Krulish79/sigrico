03The Method

Raised at sea,
always offshore

/ 01

Deep, open water

Pens sit 7 miles from shore in high-energy currents, at a depth of 330 feet — the ocean does the cleaning, naturally.

/ 02

Low density

Generous space per fish, letting them swim stronger in cleaner water around them.

/ 03

Endemic conditions

We raise each species from where it is native, helping to improve their current sustainability.

/ 04

Cold chain from day one

From sea to ice to export in hours — the freshness of the ocean, kept intact.

04Technology

Engineered for the open ocean

/ 01

Submersible cages

As a storm or red tide moves through, the cages lower 30–65 feet below the surface and ride it out — safeguarding an entire cycle.

/ 02

Marine copper mesh

Copper-alloy netting resists biofouling on its own and stays chemically inert — so the water stays pure, and its rigid form keeps turtles, dolphins and whale sharks clear.

/ 03

AI precision feeding

Biomass cameras read the fish in real time and stop feeding the instant a pellet passes uneaten — less waste, cleaner water.

/ 04

A living digital twin

Every current, temperature and load reading feeds a real-time model of the entire operation — powered offshore by sun and wave, linked by satellite.
